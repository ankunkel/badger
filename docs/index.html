Badger for Slack

Turn questions into recognized expertise with badges, points, and weekly leaderboards.

How it works

  1. Create expertise badges like React or Onboarding
  2. Ask questions tied to a badge
  3. Mark the best answer and award points
  4. See weekly leaderboards every Monday

Who it’s for

  • Engineering teams
  • Support & CX teams
  • Product & Design
  • Any team sharing knowledge in Slack

Why teams love it

  • Rewards real expertise, not noise
  • Makes knowledge visible
  • Zero dashboards — everything lives in Slack
